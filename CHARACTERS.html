CHARACTER OF THE MONTH

TY LEE

Last month, our character of the month was Azula, fitting the theme of October, the month of scares especially because of Halloween. Now we chose one of her friends and sidekicks, the very sweet Ty Lee!

Ty Lee was a cheerful and energetic girl who hailed from the Fire Nation. As the daughter of a nobleman, she attended the Royal Fire Academy for Girls with her friends, Princess Azula and Mai. Growing up, Ty Lee dealt with six sisters who shared identical appearances with her. This caused her to feel a loss of individuality and led her to have a longing to be a unique, recognizable person, and a desire for attention from other people. Because of this, after she matured, she ran away from her home and joined a Fire Nation circus, becoming a skilled acrobatic performer and gaining more personal attention. Read more.
